Our Satisfaction Guarantee

PalmShine is committed to delivering a clean you love. If you are not satisfied with the quality of your cleaning service, contact us within 24 hours of your appointment and we will return to address any missed areas — free of charge. This is our 24-Hour Shine Guarantee.

Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds or service credits in the following circumstances:

  • We are unable to fulfill a scheduled cleaning due to a PalmShine staffing issue
  • A documented quality issue is reported within 24 hours and we are unable to return to resolve it
  • A charge was made in error

Refunds are not issued for services that were completed as scheduled and where no quality concern was reported within 24 hours.

Cancellation Policy

We ask for at least 24 hours notice for any cancellation or rescheduling. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of up to 50% of the scheduled service cost.

Clean Club members may cancel their membership at any time through their customer portal or by contacting us directly. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund or report a service quality issue, contact us within 24 hours of your appointment:

  • Phone: (772) 877-5787
  • Email: management@palmshine.com

Please include your name, appointment date, address, and a brief description of the issue. We will respond within 1 business day.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5–10 business days. Service credits, if offered in lieu of a refund, will be applied to your account immediately and can be used toward any future booking.

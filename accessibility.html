Accessibility Statement

PalmShine is committed to making our website accessible to everyone.

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Our Commitment

PalmShine LLC is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continuously working to improve the accessibility of our website and services to ensure we provide equal access and equal opportunity to all users.

Conformance Status

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities. Conformance with these guidelines helps make the web more user-friendly for all people.

Measures We Take

PalmShine takes the following measures to ensure accessibility of our website:

  • Include accessibility considerations as part of our website development and design process
  • Use sufficient color contrast between text and background
  • Provide text alternatives for non-text content
  • Ensure our website is navigable using a keyboard
  • Use clear, simple language throughout our site
  • Ensure form fields have clear and descriptive labels
  • Make links descriptive so users understand their purpose

Known Limitations

While we strive to ensure accessibility across our entire website, some older content or third-party tools may not fully meet our accessibility standards. We are actively working to identify and address these issues.
